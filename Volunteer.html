Who is it?
Samuel King is a family man. He is the father of two and wants to bring him and his family to the new colony. He is currently living in suburban Detroit, aged 40. He has seen a lot in his life already and is willing to become part of the new generation part of space travel.

Goals?
Samuel King is a risk taker. He wants to be able to take risks. He has been obsessed with space travel since he was a young boy and wishes to pursue these dreams. He is going to want to live on the space colony.

Attitude?
Samuel King is going to be skeptical at first. This is something new. He is going to be both skeptical and excited for the opportunity.

Behavior
Samuel King has a very successful career that he has built on taking risks. This is a new concept as the colony is going to be the first of its' kind. Samuel is going to go through the website very carefully and make sure that this decision will be the right one for him and his family.
